What paywalls can be removed?

When it comes to paywalls, there are generally two types: hard paywalls and soft paywalls. Soft paywalls typically overlay the article, meaning the content is actually on the page but hidden behind a prompt or overlay. In contrast, hard paywalls completely restrict access to the content unless a payment is made; the article isn't present on the page until you pay. An example of a hard paywall can be seen with premium articles on sites like TechCrunch. The most effective way to determine if PaywallHub can bypass a particular paywall is simply to test the article using our tools. If the paywall isn’t removed, it’s likely a hard paywall, which are typically not bypassable through our service.
